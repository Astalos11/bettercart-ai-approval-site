# BetterCart AI Launch And Affiliate Application Checklist

Date: 2026-05-15

## Before Applying

1. Wait for `bettercartai.com` domain review / registration status to become normal.
2. Add the custom domain in Netlify:
   - `bettercartai.com`
   - `www.bettercartai.com`
3. Configure DNS records in Alibaba Cloud based on Netlify's exact instructions.
4. Wait for Netlify HTTPS certificate to become active.
5. Set Netlify environment variable:
   - `NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL=https://bettercartai.com`
6. Redeploy the site so sitemap and robots use the final domain.
7. Create or activate:
   - `contact@bettercartai.com`
8. Send a test email to confirm receiving works.

## Site Review Checklist

Before submitting an affiliate network application, check:

- Home page loads on desktop and mobile.
- Contact page shows working email.
- Affiliate Disclosure is visible.
- How We Make Money is visible.
- Privacy Policy is visible.
- Terms and Disclaimer is visible.
- Editorial Policy is visible.
- Methodology is visible.
- Partner Policy is visible.
- Program Compliance is visible.
- For Reviewers page is visible.
- Publisher Kit page is visible.
- Demo page clearly says it uses sample data.
- No fake reviews or fake traffic claims are present.
- No medical advice claims are present.

Avoid claiming:

- Medical recommendations.
- Hands-on product testing unless actually done.
- Guaranteed positive product coverage.

## Highest-Risk Review Questions

- Does the site have enough original content?
- Does the site have a working domain email?
- Does the site disclose affiliate relationships?
- Does the site look like a real publisher property rather than a thin placeholder?
- Does the site avoid unsupported health and medical claims?
