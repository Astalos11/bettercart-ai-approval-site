```text
BetterCart AI helps shoppers compare packaged foods such as snacks, protein bars, cereals, pantry items, beverages, frozen meals, sauces, and condiments. The site focuses on nutrition facts, ingredient context, serving size, category tradeoffs, and user shopping intent. Current content includes original food guides, sample comparison pages, editorial policy pages, affiliate disclosure, a publisher kit, a reviewer page, searchable site sections, and a static USDA-derived comparison demo.
```

Do not claim:

- existing affiliate partnerships
- live retailer inventory
- real-time prices
- complete product coverage
- established traffic volume
- previous affiliate sales
- fake testimonials
- hands-on product testing unless actually performed
- medical advice
- disease treatment / prevention claims
- guaranteed health outcomes
